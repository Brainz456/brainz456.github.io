"The only thing ever achieved in life without effort is failure" - Unknown

Hello and welcome to my portfolio. Throughout these pages you can find a brief outline of my skills, ambitions and effort that has gone into my education thus far. The idea of an e-portfolio is generally known, to provide a rundown of a person's professional life up to this point and while some people use their general education to focus a specific area, I have tried to work on a specific area while also trying to keep the jack of all trades approach to my other qualifications. Whenever I decide to do something, I have always strived to do the best I could possibly do; whether it be my academic qualifications or volunteering within my favourite video game community, I follow the ideal that if you are going to do something in life, it isn’t worth it unless you put all of your effort into the job at hand. This is why I chose the quote at the top of my page, when I first read this quote I felt that it resonated with me more than any quote in terms of my work ethic.
